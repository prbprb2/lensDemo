Thin Lens Demonstration

This interactive demonstration shows how a thin lens forms an image. Move the object in the GeoGebra applet to see how the image position changes.

Notice the following:

  • When the object is far away, the image forms near the focal plane.
  • When the object moves inside the focal length, the image becomes virtual.
  • The ray diagram illustrates the basic geometric optics construction.

This simple geometric construction is the basis for many imaging systems, including optical microscopes and electron microscopes. In later sections we will extend this idea to multiple lenses and to electron optical systems.
